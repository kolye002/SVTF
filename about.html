Welcome to the largest tulip festival in North America

The Skagit Valley Tulip Festival in Washington celebrates the stunning beauty of tulips in bloom. What began as a humble two-day event in 1984, has flourished into a month-long extravaganza, drawing visitors from around the world to witness nature’s colorful display. Join us in the celebration and create lasting memories amidst the beautiful landscapes of Skagit Valley!

About the Festival

The Skagit Valley Tulip Festival is a destination event for the Pacific Northwest in Washington.

The Skagit Valley Tulip Festival, an annual event held in Washington State’s picturesque Skagit Valley, celebrates the arrival of spring with millions of vibrant tulips. Throughout the month of April, visitors immerse themselves in the breathtaking beauty of blooming fields and gardens spread across the county. From RoozenGaarde’s expansive 50-acre display to the charm of Tulip Town, Garden Rosalyn, and Tulip Valley Farms, each destination offers a unique experience.

With diverse events and activities, including art shows, a street fair, and even a Pickleball Tournament, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Plan your visit by checking bloom updates, exploringticket options for individual gardens, and our calendar of events.

Don’t miss the chance to witness this colorful spectacle and create lasting memories during the only Washington Tulip Festival. We are proud to be the largest Tulip Festival in North America!
